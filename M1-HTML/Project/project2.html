Cristiano Ronaldo

Early Life

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He grew up in a poor family and discovered his passion for football at a very young age.

Career

Ronaldo started his professional career at Sporting CP. He then moved to Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and later Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia. He is one of the greatest footballers of all time with over 900 career goals.

Awards

He has won the Ballon d'Or five times and the UEFA Champions League five times. He also led Portugal to victory at UEFA Euro 2016.

Legacy

Cristiano Ronaldo is known for his incredible work ethic, speed, and goal-scoring ability. He is one of the most followed people on social media with over 600 million followers and is a true global icon.
