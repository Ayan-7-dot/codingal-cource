SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is a sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea, in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and is known for his boundless optimism, enthusiasm, and childlike wonder.

Key Facts

  • Species: Sea Sponge
  • Home: A pineapple
  • Job: Fry Cook at the Krusty Krab
  • Personality: Cheerful, energetic, and endlessly optimistic

Friends

  • Patrick Star - his best friend, a starfish neighbor
  • Squidward Tentacles - his neighbor, often annoyed by him
  • Sandy Cheeks - a squirrel scientist who lives in a dome
  • Mr. Krabs - his boss at the Krusty Krab

Famous Quote

"I'm ready! I'm ready! I'm ready!"
